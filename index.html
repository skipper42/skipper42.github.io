About Me

I am a graduate of Faculty of Engineering, Alexandria University, Electronics and Communications section.
I started working at Orange. I was an ATM implementation engineer, then I moved on to Security Technical support, also Orange. My scope included Checkpoint and Juniper Firewalls, Bluecoat and Ironport proxies, UTMs, Antiviruses, F5 load balancers and more. the job also covered many messaging solutions including postfix smtp relays and exchange servers. I then moved to IBM systemX hardware support, I was supporting hardware servers including IBM X series, Blades and Power Blades, Flex and Pure Flex. I didnt spend much time in IBM (I hated the place), and then I moved to RSA.
I am currently working as Netwitness support engineer for RSA Security.
